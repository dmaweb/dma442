2019 referee courses
Attention GISC Soccer players (Ages 12 and up) – provided below is the link for Referee training/certification courses being hosted locally in Western NY over the next few weeks.

Several courses are being offered locally in December to March 2019 time frame.

For more information please visit: www.wnyreferee.org

Please note that instructions for registration and information is also provided on this site. Important to note – In Order To Referee for Grand Island Soccer Club you MUST be FIFA Ref certified. This certification will also allow you to referee in BWNYJSL games.

Thank you to all who have inquired as to ongoing FIFA Referee Training programs – as available locally in Western NY. We look forward to seeing you on the fields in the coming season.
